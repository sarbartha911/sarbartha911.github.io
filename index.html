Specialisations

          
I provide end-to-end advisory and delivery support for enterprise data transformation programmes, with strong focus on measurable quality outcomes, governance maturity, and low-risk migration execution.

        


        

          

            

            
Business & Data Discovery

            
Lead structured discovery with business and technology stakeholders to baseline current-state data, define migration scope, and align programme outcomes with business priorities.

          

          

            

            
Migration Architecture & Planning

            
Design migration blueprints, cutover and rollback plans, reconciliation checkpoints, and business sign-off criteria to support predictable and auditable delivery.

          

          

            

            
Risk, Controls & Assurance

            
Identify risks early, define mitigation controls, and run impact assessments across data, process, and platform dependencies to reduce delivery and operational exposure.

          

          

            

            
DQ, Governance & MDM

            
Establish data quality rulebooks, stewardship workflows, and MDM golden record models using Informatica IDMC/MDM SaaS, Collibra, and enterprise governance patterns.

          

          

            

            
Data Profiling & Mapping

            
Execute deep source profiling and source-to-target mapping with SQL and metadata-driven approaches to improve transformation accuracy and traceability.

          

          

            

            
Python Automation & Reconciliation

            
Build Python-based accelerators for reconciliation, control reporting, and exception analytics to improve speed, quality, and migration confidence at scale.
